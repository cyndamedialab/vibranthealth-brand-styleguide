Marketing Styles

Since 1992, Vibrant Health has been committed to a standard of "Full Transparency," intentionally avoiding the industry trend of "proprietary blends" that hide actual dosages. By disclosing every single ingredient and its exact amount, we ensure you know exactly what is fueling your body. Our mission is simple: to provide fresh looks and the same trusted formulas that support grateful hearts and healthy lives.

Key Marketing Pillars

Below are the new marketing pillars for the rebrand. These should be reviewed and perhaps revised in 12 to 18 months.

Pillar 1

Full Transparency in Every Scoop

We explicitly list every ingredient and its exact amount instead of using "proprietary blends" to mask our formulations.

Pillar 2

The World's First Green Powder

Our legacy is built on over three decades of commitment to quality and clinical excellence.

Pillar 3

Same Trusted Formulas, Fresh Look

While we embrace a "Fresh Look," we guarantee the "Same Trusted Formulas" that our community has relied on for years.

Marketing Style #1: Brand Story

Purpose

Build brand connection and awareness.

Crystal Clear. Trusted Always.

How might we show transparency and build trust through visual storytelling?

Example Topics

  • Product spotlight with benefit highlights
  • Behind-the-scenes stories (sourcing ingredients, supplier stories)
  • Customer stories or testimonials
  • "Vibrant Truth" transparency messages
Core Concepts
Clarity, Purity, and Trust
Design Sensibility
The overall design sensibility should feel science-driven and precise, with layouts that are clean, elevated, and reminiscent of high-end brands. Embrace a “less is more” approach—prioritizing clarity, intentional spacing, and a calm visual rhythm. Every element should have a purpose, allowing the design to feel refined, confident, and effortlessly sophisticated.
Visual Style

The overall visual style should reinforce Vibrant Health's commitment to transparency and trust, drawing a clear parallel to the simplicity of a glass of water.

Use strong natural lighting for environmental product shots or product-in-use shots to create luminous scenes with crisp, defined shadows. The light should always evoke sunlight to convey freshness and authenticity.

Incorporate transparent objects, shadows, and reflective surfaces to enhance the sense of purity and clarity.

Look & Feel
Clean, bright, high-key (white or light backgrounds), and minimalist.
Aesthetic Goal
Sophisticated, honest, and scientifically grounded, while still feeling fresh and accessible.

Marketing Style #2: Vibrant Lifestyle

Purpose

Inspire, educate, and add value to followers' lives.

Example Topics

  • Nutrition tips (e.g., “5 ways to boost your morning energy”)
  • Fitness and recovery routines
  • Gut health and microbiome facts
  • Smoothie recipes
  • Expert Q&A snippets or myth-busting posts...etc.
Core Concepts
Bright, Optimistic, and Real
Design Sensibility
The overall design sensibility should echo the polished, aspirational feel of a lifestyle magazine. Use a frosted-glass effect to enhance readability when necessary, and incorporate the brand's signature pattern to elevate the visual experience. Do not force the product into every shot. Vibrant Health champions a healthy lifestyle, not product-centric imagery driven by profit.
Visual Style

Subjects should never appear posed or self-conscious. Capture candid, in-between moments—stretching, laughing, prepping a green mix, or simply enjoying the sun.

Use strong natural lighting to create luminous scenes with crisp, defined shadows. The lighting should always evoke sunlight to communicate freshness and authenticity.

Smiles should feel genuine, eyes bright, and gestures fluid. Prioritize emotional connection over perfection. Models should not pose for the camera; instead, they should be captured in natural, everyday interactions with the product.

Look & Feel
The imagery should capture real, spontaneous moments that celebrate health, movement, and joy.
Aesthetic Goal
Every photo should feel alive with genuine warmth and optimism, reflecting Vibrant Health’s belief that wellness is not a routine but a lifestyle.

Marketing Style #3: Special Topic

Purpose

Timely, connective, and distinct

Special topic posts should feel timely, celebratory, and distinct—whether marking a holiday, campaign, or cultural moment. Use motion, texture, or festive accents to add freshness while maintaining the brand’s refined tone, creating visual moments that connect Vibrant Health with its community.

Purpose
Activate VH IG Fan Base (Gen X) / Aquire New Followers (Millennials and Older Gen-Z)
Concept

Everyone’s in the "greens" game now. Everyone’s "discovering" supergreens, but VH was here before hashtags, influencers, and TikTok wellness hacks.

We’re celebrating our legacy and calling out the hype while owning our authenticity. This is a confident, slightly rebellious love letter to our roots. We didn’t jump on the bandwagon — we built it.

Message

So much has changed since 1992 — except our commitment to your health.

As we prepare to launch our new brand design, we invite you to honor the past and celebrate the future with us.

Visual Style — Nostalgic Newtro
"Nostalgic Newtro" refers to a trend that combines the words "new" and "retro" to describe a contemporary interpretation of past styles and culture. It offers a nostalgic feeling for older generations while presenting past eras as a fresh novelty to younger generations, appealing to a desire for authenticity and emotional stability amidst digital fatigue.
Mechanic

Vibrant Health posts a series of short, playful "Then vs. Now" comparisons weekly using the hashtag #Since1992 and #ThrowbackThursday.

To participate, simply add #Since1992 and tag @VibrantHealth

Incentive

Weekly reposts of fan submissions on Vibrant Health’s channels and pre-launch landing page.

A "Then & Now" giveaway: Participants can win a Vibrant Health Vintage Starter Pack — classic tee, new product bundle, and custom retro tote.
