Color System

The Vibrant Health label color system is built from a single Pantone-driven spectrum that maps every SKU to a distinct color story. Each product owns a primary hue plus a supporting palette of accents, ensuring a unified family while keeping individual products instantly recognizable on the shelf.

Full Spectrum

The master Pantone spectrum every product label is sampled from. Use these values for all printed packaging — never substitute screen-only hex values for print artwork.

Label Breakdowns

Where each color from the assigned palette appears on the label. Use this map when laying out new SKUs to keep typographic hierarchy and regulatory zones consistent.

View Label Parts
Background Color
Sets the dominant identity of the SKU on the shelf — primary product hue.
Logo Motif
Color treatment used for the molecular logo motif on the label face.
Motif Bars
Repeating accent bars carrying secondary product color across the panel.
Burst Circle
Highlight callout ("new", "updated formula", "flavor") — uses the accent color.
Burst Divider
Divider between primary and secondary color zones inside the burst.
Product Title
Primary wordmark color — selected for shelf legibility against the background.
Pill-shaped Callout
Sub-line color used for flavor or claim pills below the product title.
Supplement Facts — Background
Background color for the regulatory facts panel.
Supplement Facts — Ingredient Header
Color of the bold header inside the supplement facts table.
Side Panel Divider
Vertical divider used along the side panels to anchor secondary content.
Text-Dark
Dark text treatment used when the panel background is light.
Text-Light
Light text treatment used when the panel background is dark.
