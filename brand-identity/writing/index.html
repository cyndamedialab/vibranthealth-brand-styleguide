Writing

Our written voice is as carefully crafted as our visual identity. Direct, credible, and grounded in science — these guidelines ensure every word, from product labels to social captions, carries the same clarity and warmth that defines Vibrant Health. No overclaiming. No jargon. Just honest communication.

Brand Archetype

Vibrant Health is the Hero and the Magician archetype, utilizing both to create a more dynamic, multi-dimensional, and relatable personality. At our core identity, we are the Hero, and the Magician is "supporting." We balance the two — leading with the Hero to build trust and motivation, then using the Magician to provide the "wow" factor and the scientific "why."

✓ Do
  • Speak to the customer's ambition.
  • Emphasize that results are earned through the Hero's effort but unlocked by our Magician's science.
✕ Don't
  • Don't be overly clinical or cold; science should feel like a superpower, not a textbook.
  • Don't use "fluff" or "magic" in a way that sounds like pseudoscience; the Magician's power comes from unique insights and proven data.
