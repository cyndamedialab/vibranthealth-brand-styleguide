Photography

Photography defines how we visualize our world, focusing on lighting, purposeful composition, and authentic subjects to build an instant, human connection with our audience.

Product Photo Guidelines Lifestyle Photo Guidelines

Core Concept: Clarity, Purity, and Trust

The overall visual style should reinforce Vibrant Health's commitment to transparency and trust, drawing a clear parallel to the image of a simple glass of water.

Look & Feel

Clean, bright, high-key (white or bright backgrounds), and minimalist.

Aesthetic Goal

Sophisticated, honest, and scientifically grounded, while also feeling fresh and accessible.

Product Packaging Shots

Lighting
Use soft, even, and bright studio lighting to eliminate harsh shadows and create a "crystal-clear" look. Incorporate backlighting to enhance the transparency of liquids and objects.
Focus
Ensure pin-sharp focus on the main subject. Use a shallow depth of field for product shots to subtly blur the background and make the product stand out.
Color Palette
Primary colors should come from vibrant, natural ingredient tones (kale, beets, smoothies) to communicate freshness and vitality. Secondary tones include clean whites, transparent glass, and clear water to communicate purity and transparency.
Setup
Shoot products on a pure white or reflective glass/acrylic surface to evoke the clarity of a water glass. Keep the setup distraction-free.
Angle
Use direct, straight-on shots or a slight low angle to convey integrity and prominence.
Detail
Ensure all text, including product names (e.g., GREEN VIBRANCE, MAXIMUM VIBRANCE) and key benefit callouts (e.g., 25 Billion Probiotics, 65 Functional Foods), is legible and crisp.

Ingredient Shots

Water Glass Metaphor
Use the water glass as a visual metaphor — universally recognized for clarity, purity, and honesty. Shoot against a bright white background with a subtle reflection for maximum effect.
Ingredient Focus
Capture ingredients on a clean surface such as a petri dish or white background to emphasize purity and natural integrity.

Purpose

These images reinforce the idea that there are "no hidden ingredients and no surprises," promoting trust and transparency.

Prepared Product Shots — Smoothies & Ingredient Showcase

Liquids & Texture
Highlight the vibrancy and richness of the prepared drinks and ingredients. Green smoothies and ingredients should appear fresh, appetizing, and full of life.
Contextual Styling
Arrange shots (such as the four-drink lineup) to feel aspirational and health-focused. Include whole, fresh ingredients (kale, lemons, grapefruits) to link visual storytelling to benefits like Nutrition, Energy, and Immunity.
Background
Use muted, light-colored backgrounds (pale blue or green) that complement the drink colors without overpowering them. Avoid cluttered or busy backgrounds.

Purpose

Bring more energy and visual impact through richer colors and more dynamic textures.

Prepared Product Shots — Lifestyle Setting

Lighting & Angles
Use natural or soft diffused light with considered angles and subtle shadows to create depth and sophistication.
Styling
Keep surfaces clean, structured, and minimal — bright stone or granite countertops to reinforce a modern, elevated aesthetic.
Composition
Use angles from the environment and shadow to establish a modern and sophisticated aesthetic.
Mood
The overall impression should feel fresh, healthy, and attainable, capturing the idea that Vibrant Health is part of everyday wellness.

Purpose

Show prepared products naturally integrated into real-life environments — kitchen countertop, breakfast table — to convey authenticity and approachability.

✕ Avoid

Keep composition minimalistic and stylish. Avoid overly staged setups or cluttered home settings that distract from the product's purity and message of trust.
