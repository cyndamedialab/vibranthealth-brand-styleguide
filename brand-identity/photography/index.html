Photography

Photography defines how we visualize our world, focusing on lighting, purposeful composition, and authentic subjects to build an instant, human connection with our audience.

Core Concept: Clarity, Purity, and Trust

The overall visual style should reinforce Vibrant Health's commitment to transparency and trust, drawing a clear parallel to the image of a simple glass of water.

Look & Feel

Clean, bright, high-key (white or bright backgrounds), and minimalist.

Aesthetic Goal

Sophisticated, honest, and scientifically grounded, while also feeling fresh and accessible.

Product Packaging Shots

Lighting
Use soft, even, and bright studio lighting to eliminate harsh shadows and create a "crystal-clear" look. Incorporate backlighting to enhance the transparency of liquids and objects.
Focus
Ensure pin-sharp focus on the main subject. Use a shallow depth of field for product shots to subtly blur the background and make the product stand out.
Color Palette
Primary colors should come from vibrant, natural ingredient tones (kale, beets, smoothies) to communicate freshness and vitality, complementing the Vibrant Health brand. Secondary tones include clean whites, transparent glass, and clear water to communicate purity and transparency.
Setup
Shoot products on a pure white or reflective glass/acrylic surface to evoke the clarity of a water glass. Keep the setup distraction-free.
Angle
Use direct, straight-on shots or a slight low angle to convey integrity and prominence.
Detail
Ensure all text, including product names (e.g., GREEN VIBRANCE, MAXIMUM VIBRANCE) and key benefit callouts (e.g., 25 Billion Probiotics, 65 Functional Foods), is legible and crisp.

Ingredient Shots

Water Glass Metaphor
Use the water glass as a visual metaphor — universally recognized for clarity, purity, and honesty. Shoot against a bright white background with a subtle reflection for maximum effect.
Ingredient Focus
Capture ingredients on a clean surface such as a petri dish or white background to emphasize purity and natural integrity.

Purpose

These images reinforce the idea that there are "no hidden ingredients and no surprises," promoting trust and transparency.

Prepared Product Shots — Smoothies & Ingredient Showcase

Liquids & Texture
Highlight the vibrancy and richness of the prepared drinks and or ingredients. Green smoothies and ingredients should appear fresh, appetizing, and full of life.
Contextual Styling
Arrange shots (such as the four-drink lineup) to feel aspirational and health-focused. Include whole, fresh ingredients (e.g., kale, lemons, grapefruits) to link visual storytelling to benefits like Nutrition, Energy, and Immunity.
Background
Use muted, light-colored backgrounds (such as pale blue or green) that complement the drink colors without overpowering them. Avoid cluttered or busy backgrounds.

Purpose

Bring more energy and visual impact through richer colors and more dynamic textures.

Prepared Product Shots — Lifestyle Setting

Lighting & Angles
Use natural or soft diffused light with considered angles and subtle shadows to create depth and sophistication.
Styling
Keep surfaces clean, structured, and minimal — bright stone or granite countertops to reinforce a modern, elevated aesthetic.
Composition
Use angles from the environment and shadow to establish a modern and sophisticated aesthetic.
Mood
The overall impression should feel fresh, healthy, and attainable, capturing the idea that Vibrant Health is part of everyday wellness.

Purpose

Show prepared products naturally integrated into real-life environments, such as a kitchen countertop or breakfast table, to convey authenticity and approachability.

X Avoid

Keep the composition and visual style minimalistic and stylish, yet overly staged setups or cluttered home settings that distract from the product’s purity and message of trust.

Visual Mood

Candid, energetic, and full of vitality

The imagery should capture real, spontaneous moments that celebrate health, movement, and joy. Every photo should feel alive with genuine warmth and optimism that reflects Vibrant Health’s belief that wellness is not a routine, but a lifestyle.

Natural light

Use strong natural lighting to create luminous scenes with crisp, defined shadows. Light should always feel like sunlight: radiant and full of life to evoke freshness, energy, and authenticity. Avoid artificial or overly stylized setups.

Mood and Vibe

  • Bright
  • Optimistic
  • Real (not staged)
  • Fresh

Color Palette

Base tones

Clean whites and soft neutrals to ground the composition.

Accent tones

Vibrant hues drawn from nature such as greens, citrus tones, sunlit yellows, ocean blues, and terracotta warmth. The goal is to convey vitality and diversity.

Skin tones

Natural, inclusive, and unretouched to reflect honesty and human connection. Diversity of age, ethnicity, and lifestyle is essential to showing the full spectrum of health.

Composition

Casual and Spontaneous Framing
Composition should feel unposed and natural, like a moment caught mid-laughter or reflection. Imperfection adds credibility and warmth.
Depth and Focus
Use a shallow depth of field to emphasize intimacy and connection. Keep key subjects in crisp focus while allowing backgrounds to softly blur, creating a sense of depth and calm energy.
Movement and Rhythm
Subtle motion such as wind in hair, someone stretching, water being poured to help express vibrancy and vitality. Whenever possible, avoid overly symmetrical setups, which may appear stable but lack rhythm.
Editorial Realism
Frame like a modern lifestyle magazine: approachable, human, and story-driven. Include details that tell a story about moments in life such as a smoothie on a countertop, sunlight streaming through leaves, or a shared laugh at the breakfast table.
Angle
Use direct, straight-on shots or a slight low angle to convey integrity and prominence.
Detail
Ensure all text, including product names (e.g., GREEN VIBRANCE, MAXIMUM VIBRANCE) and key benefit callouts (e.g., 25 Billion Probiotics, 65 Functional Foods), is legible and crisp.

Environment

Sunlit, lived-in spaces

Capture bright indoor or outdoor scenes that feel real and relatable: kitchens, home gyms, patios, gardens, or parks. Settings should feel modern and airy, with natural textures and uncluttered design.

Connection to nature

Incorporate greenery, water, or organic elements whenever possible to reinforce the brand's connection to natural wellness. Avoid sterile, staged, or overly commercial backdrops.

Gestures & Expression

Authenticity First
Subjects should never look posed or self-conscious. Capture them in candid, in-between moments—stretching, laughing, prepping a green mix, or simply enjoying the sun. When a product is shown with a model, it should depict authentic use. Avoid staged or promotional presentation to keep moments genuine and natural.
Expressions
Smiles should feel genuine, eyes bright, and gestures fluid. Prioritize emotional connection over perfection. The model should not be posing for the camera; instead, they should be captured in natural, everyday engagement with the product.
Interactions
Encourage scenes of togetherness: friends running outdoors, families sharing meals, couples relaxing post-workout. The goal is to convey holistic wellness and the joy of connection.

✕ Avoid

  • Exaggerated fitness poses or scenes (we are not Huel)
  • Party-like or performative happiness (we are not Bloom)
  • Symmetrical compositions with a clear central focus point and a clinical feel (we are not AG1)
