Brand Overview

Vibrant Health is a family-owned company founded in 1992. This section captures who we are, what drives us, and the values that define everything we create. Use these principles to ensure every design decision aligns with our brand character.

History

Vibrant Health is a family-owned company, founded in 1992 with the launch of Green Vibrance — the world's first green superfood formula. From the beginning, we've done things differently: no proprietary blends, no hidden doses. Every single ingredient and its exact milligram amount is listed on the label. Every product is triple-tested and third-party verified. This commitment to Full Disclosure Labeling wasn't a marketing strategy — it was a moral stance. We believe people deserve to know exactly what they're putting into their bodies.

Mission

Why do we do what we do?

We care about people and believe everyone deserves access to honest, effective nutrition grounded in truth, not marketing.

What problems are we trying to solve?

People want simple solutions to support their health, yet are often confused by marketing jargon.

What changes would we like to see in the world or our industry?

Radical Transparency: because people deserve to know exactly what they're putting into their bodies. #VibrantTruth

The VH Golden Circle

Simon Sinek's Golden Circle provides a framework for communicating from the inside out — starting with why we exist, not what we sell. For Vibrant Health, this means leading every touchpoint with our commitment to truth and human empowerment, letting our products and processes follow naturally from that purpose.

"People don't buy what you do; they buy why you do it." — Simon Sinek

WHAT

Product/Result

Vibrant Health is a family-owned company, founded in 1992 with the launch of Green Vibrance, the world's first green superfood formula.

With a core mission to empower health through truth and transparency, Vibrant Health offers a range of highly awarded, rigorously tested nutritional supplements, committing to full ingredient disclosure and continuous formula improvements.

HOW

Process/Values

Full Disclosure Labeling: Every single ingredient and its exact milligram amount is listed on the label.

Rigorous Testing: Every product is triple-tested and third-party verified to meet strict GMP standards.

Dynamic By Design: The product formula updates regularly to reflect the latest nutritional science.

Truth. Trust. Transparency.™ Every product is made with fully disclosed ingredients: no blends, no fluff.

WHY

Core/Purpose

We care about people and believe everyone deserves access to honest, effective nutrition grounded in truth, not marketing.

From the beginning, we've done things differently. No proprietary blends. No cutting corners.

We're not perfect, but we're honest. And we're always working to do better.

Design Statement

Our design embodies Molecular Transformation — visually representing the brand's evolution through the dynamic interplay of organic forms and structured geometry. Inspired by the fluidity of nature and the precision of science, it bridges Vibrant Health's heritage with its forward-thinking vision.

Brand Archetype

Vibrant Health operates with a dual archetype — a blend of two Jungian frameworks that define our emotional and aspirational language.

The Hero

Courage through knowledge

Inspires and motivates. Focuses on achievement, courage, and overcoming challenges. For Vibrant Health, this means empowering individuals to conquer their health goals through the proven science behind our products.

The Magician

Science as transformation

Brings about transformation, making the impossible possible through unique insights. For Vibrant Health, this represents how our products work — turning molecular science into visible, felt results.
